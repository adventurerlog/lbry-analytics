* /reports/content?auth_token=`token`&claim_id=`id_tied_with_token`

### Reward per view for XYZ channel

* /user_rewards/view_rate?auth_token=`token`

* /subscription/sub_count?auth_token=`token`&claim_id=`channel_claim_id`
